What Gary offers

Electrical work done right, first time.

Whether it's a single socket or a full consumer unit upgrade, every job gets Gary's full attention.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Replace your fuse board with a modern, safe consumer unit — required for insurance, rewires, and adding new circuits. All notifiable work self-certified under Part P.

Fault Finding & Repairs

Strange trip-outs, dead sockets, flickering lights? Gary tracks down faults and fixes them properly — not a quick bodge that fails again in a month.

Lighting Installation & Upgrades

Indoor and garden lighting from pendant rewires to full LED upgrades — installed to look and work right, first time.

Socket & Switch Installation

Extra sockets where you need them, USB sockets, relocations, and switch upgrades — all Part P certified, all done neatly.

EICR Certificates

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for homeowners and landlords. Completed efficiently, report delivered promptly. Essential for rentals and property sales.

Extractor Fan Installation

Kitchen and bathroom extractor fans — new installs, replacements, and repairs, all wired to regulation and working as they should.

Kitchen & Extension Electrics

New circuits, cooker points, sockets and lighting for extensions and kitchen refits. All notifiable work self-certified under Part P — you get the paperwork.

Smoke Detector Installation

Mains-wired, interlinked smoke and heat detectors — the right protection for your home, fitted by a registered competent person.

A note on notifiable work

As a Part P registered competent person, Gary can self-certify notifiable electrical work — consumer unit replacements, new circuits, and work in special locations like bathrooms — without you needing to separately notify your local authority. Every job comes with the right paperwork. If you're a landlord, Gary can also provide the EICR your tenants need.

Reliability, written down.

We’re not the cheapest quote you’ll get — and we’re fine with that. We’re the team you call when you want the job scoped clearly, done once, and certified properly. Here’s what happens every time.

  1. 01

    You get in touch

    Call, email, or use the form. Our office manager handles bookings during working hours and will suggest the next available slot that fits the job.

  2. 02

    We visit and assess

    Daniel or a qualified electrician attends, listens to what you need, and inspects what’s there — especially important in older Cotswold stone properties where wiring rarely runs in straight lines.

  3. 03

    Written quote the same day

    By 7pm you’ll have a clear quote in your inbox — not a verbal figure you have to chase. No surprises, no “we’ll send it next week.”

  4. 04

    Work, certification, guarantee

    We complete notifiable work under Part P, issue your certificate, and stand behind installations with a 12-month workmanship guarantee.

About

A small team that treats your home like we live there too.

Ridgecoat came together after years of crossing paths on larger Bristol refurbishments — two lead decorators who preferred straight answers, tidy sites, and finishes that still look right six months later.

Our story

We formalised the partnership in 2019 after running parallel crews for different contractors — same city, same standards, and a shared dislike of vague quotes. Between us there’s over 20 years of combined decorating experience, mostly in period terraces, flats, and busy family homes around Bristol.

Work has come from referrals, repeat customers, and the usual trade directories — not because we’re the loudest name online, but because we keep the basics solid: clear pricing, realistic schedules, and a site that stays livable while we’re in it.

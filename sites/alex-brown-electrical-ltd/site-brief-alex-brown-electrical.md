## 6. Visual direction
**Aesthetic direction:**
Clean and confident — the visual equivalent of a tidy van and a job done right. Not a corporate blue-and-yellow template. Think dark background, sharp white type, a single amber or copper accent that nods to the trade without being clichéd. The Westbury market is domestic-heavy; homeowners want someone who looks trustworthy and professional, not flashy.

**Colour direction:**
Deep charcoal or near-black base (authority, seriousness of the trade — this is safety-critical work). Warm amber or amber-gold accent (trade warmth, filament-bulb nod). White for body copy and trust elements. Avoid the generic "electrician blue" — every competitor uses it.

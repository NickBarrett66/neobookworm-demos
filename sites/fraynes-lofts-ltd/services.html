Services

The kind of work we’re asked for most.

We’ll tell you what’s possible, what’s sensible, and what it’s likely to cost—before anything gets complicated.

Dormer loft conversions

The popular choice when you want proper, usable space—more headroom, more floor area, better layout options.

Good for: Terraced and semi-detached homes across Bath and Wiltshire.

Includes structure, insulation, windows, staircase, plastering and finish to “ready to decorate” (subject to final scope).

Velux / rooflight conversions

A lighter-touch option when the existing roofline can stay. Bright rooms, lower build complexity, great value where headroom already works.

Hip-to-gable conversions

Turns a hipped roof into a full gable end—one of the biggest space gains, often the best return for the right house type.

Loft bedrooms (with storage)

A bedroom that feels like it was always meant to be there—proper staircase, sensible storage in the eaves, clean lines.

Often paired with: an en-suite (below).

Loft en-suites

Compact, well-planned, properly vented. Tiling done right. Details finished cleanly.

Home office loft conversions

A quiet room that works year-round—insulation, heating plan, power and lighting where you actually need it.

House extensions (single & double-storey)

Rear and side extensions from foundations up, built to fit the house rather than looking “added on”.

Joinery & staircases (part of loft projects)

Stairs are where loft conversions feel cheap or feel right. We make sure they feel right.

One person accountable.

You’ll speak to Olly. You’ll see Olly. If something needs deciding, you won’t be waiting for a “site manager” you’ve never met.

Specialist trades are booked and managed as part of the job—electrician, plumber and plasterer—so you’re not coordinating four calendars yourself.

What we don’t do

We focus on loft conversions and extensions. If you’re after general handyman work or tiny patch jobs, we’ll usually recommend someone better suited.

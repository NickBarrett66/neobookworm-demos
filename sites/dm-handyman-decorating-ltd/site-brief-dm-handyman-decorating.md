## 6. Visual direction
**Aesthetic direction:**  
Honest craft. Not slick or corporate — this is a one-man trade site that should look confident and solid without pretending to be a firm. Think clean layouts with real-feeling textures: painted surfaces, plaster walls, a freshly brushed finish. Warm but not cosy. Competent, not flashy.

**Colour direction:**  
Warm off-whites and deep slate or charcoal, with a brushed warm mid-tone (think fresh emulsion, not paint-chart-sample blue). Avoids anything clinical. The palette should feel like a well-decorated room, not a corporate template.

**Reference images or sites (optional):**  
None

**Typography direction:**  
Display: something with a bit of weight and character — a slab serif or confident grotesque that feels workman-proud, not startup-cute. Body: clean, readable, comfortable at paragraph length. No Inter. No Roboto. Something like Bitter + Source Sans, or Playfair Display + Lato if going warmer.

**Aesthetic direction:** <mark>Grounded and earthy, with clean modern structure. Not a generic trades template — lean into the Forest of Dean character: deep woodland, stone, timber. The site should feel local and personal, not like a national franchise. Clean whitespace, strong typography, natural palette. Confident but not flashy.</mark>
**Colour direction:** <mark>Forest green as the lead colour (deep, #1D3A28 range — not lime, not corporate teal). Warm off-white/cream background (#F8F5EE). Amber or warm honey as CTA accent (#C07A2A range). Stone mid-tone for secondary text and borders. Avoid blues entirely — they read as corporate and national.</mark>
**Reference images or sites (optional):** <mark>Avoid the look of forestofdeanhandyman.com (functional but flat) and handy-property-services.co.uk (generic template). Aim for the feel of a well-made rural artisan brand — think of a good quality local farm shop or village builder — without being fussy.</mark>
**Typography direction:** <mark>Work Sans or Cabin for headings — sturdy, clean, readable, slightly informal without being casual. Lora or Source Serif for body text — warm and readable. Avoid Playfair Display (too decorative for this trade). The hierarchy should feel workmanlike: clear, purposeful, nothing superfluous.</mark>

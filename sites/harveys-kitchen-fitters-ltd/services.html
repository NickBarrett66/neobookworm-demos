Our Services

What Harvey's Cover

Full Kitchen Supply & Fit

We source quality units and handle the full installation — one team, one invoice.

Whether you're starting from scratch or replacing an existing kitchen, we manage supply through our trade accounts — including Howdens Joinery — and handle every stage of the fit. You choose the style and finish; we handle the rest.

Fit-Only Service

Already chosen your units? We'll install them to the same standard regardless of where you bought them.

Flat-pack from IKEA, a bespoke range from a local joinery, a Howdens order you've placed yourself — we'll fit it properly and leave the kitchen looking like it was always there.

Full Project Management

Plumbing, electrics, tiling — we coordinate every trade so you have one point of contact, not six.

Most kitchen fitters fit kitchens. Harvey's manage the whole job. We've built trade relationships across Bath over 25 years — we coordinate the sequence, the access, and the scheduling so the job runs clean.

Contract & Developer Work

Multi-unit projects for developers and landlords, completed on time and without drama.

We work with developers on new builds and refurbishments across Bath and Somerset. Reliable, trade-standard installation at scale — with the same family business accountability you get on a domestic job.

Design & Layout Advice

Twenty-five years of Bath kitchens means we know what works before a single unit is ordered.

Victorian terraces, Georgian townhouses, new-build apartments — we've worked in every Bath property type. We'll advise on layout, flow, and practical constraints before anything is committed to order. No charge for early conversations.

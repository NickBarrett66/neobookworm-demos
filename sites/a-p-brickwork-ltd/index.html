Permeable doesn't have to be complicated.

Planning and drainage rules increasingly expect homeowners to use responsible surfacing. SuDS regulations — Sustainable Drainage Systems — affect more driveways than most people realise, especially in built-up areas like Reading and Berkshire.

A P Brickwork builds permeable and compliant solutions as standard practice, not as a premium add-on. We explain what's required in plain English and build accordingly — no upselling jargon, no shortcuts in the sub-base where most driveways quietly fail years later.

"Block paving done right starts with the sub-base — we don't cut corners there, because that's where most driveways fail."

Why permeable surfacing matters

  • Impermeable surfaces over 5 m² typically require planning permission — permeable ones don't.
  • Compliant drainage reduces flood risk for the whole street — not just your property.
  • Well-specified permeable block paving lasts as long as conventional — and looks just as good.

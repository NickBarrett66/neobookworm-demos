What we do

Services

Everything starts with preparation — excavation, levels, and drainage — before any paving goes down.

Three core services

No subcontracting, no compromises. We do the groundwork ourselves.

Permeable & SuDS-compliant driveways

Block paving, resin bound, and gravel systems installed with drainage and regulation expectations in mind. Compliant surfacing increasingly matters — new driveways over 5 m² require planning permission if they don't allow water to drain naturally. We build it right from the start.

Materials: permeable block paving, resin bound aggregate, angular gravel, geotextile membrane where required.

Block paving driveways & patios

New installs and replacements with neat edging, proper falls, and clean joints. Block paving is only as good as what's underneath — compacted sub-base, adequate bedding, and correct falls to prevent standing water. We don't skip those stages.

Options: concrete block, clay brick, tegula, tumbled — pattern and colour to suit your property.

Brickwork & groundworks

Walls, edging, and foundations — the structural work that keeps paving stable for years. Boundary walls, retaining walls, garden steps, and kerbing laid to correct levels. We also handle the excavation and disposal that every new driveway or patio needs before the build can begin.

Work includes: excavation, soil removal, concrete foundations, brick or block walling, kerbstone edging.

How we work

1

Site assessment

We visit the site, measure up, discuss drainage requirements, and agree on materials and approach before any price is given.

2

Excavation & sub-base

Existing surface removed and disposed of. Ground excavated to the correct depth. Hardcore sub-base compacted in layers — this is where longevity is built in.

3

Drainage & bedding

Drainage channels, gullies, or permeable membranes laid as specified. Bedding sand or mortar prepared to correct falls for water runoff.

4

Paving & finishing

Blocks or flags laid to pattern, cut accurately at edges, jointed and compacted. Site cleaned and left tidy. We don't walk away until it's right.

## How to update this tracker

**In a typical session** (this is the easy path — one edit, done):

1. Open the **Status at a glance** table at the top
2. Change the **Current phase** cell for the site you worked on
3. If the site just started, change **Status** from `Not started` to `In progress`
4. If the site just went live, change **Status** to `Complete`, set **Current phase** to `—`, and fill in the **Live URL**
5. Commit: *tracker: `<site-name>` → `<new phase>`*

**Occasionally, when you want to reflect fuller detail:**

1. Scroll to the per-site section
2. Update phase ticks, notes, or dates
3. Commit the same way

You don't have to update the per-site phase grids on every session — they're there when you want them, not as a requirement.

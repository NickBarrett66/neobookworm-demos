## How to update this tracker

- At the end of each work session, tick any phase boxes completed
- Update the per-site **Current phase** line
- When a site goes live, fill in the **Live URL** and **Completed** date
- Add anything unusual to the site's **Notes** — blockers, decisions, links to relevant LEARNINGS entries
- Commit the updated tracker with message: *tracker: update `<site-name>` to phase `<n>`*

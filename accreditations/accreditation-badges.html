Usage notes
  • Demo sites: Use placeholder reg numbers and review counts — they're clearly fictional.
  • Live client sites: Ask the client for their actual accreditation logos from their member portal — Gas Safe, NICEIC, FENSA, TrustMark all provide official files to registered members.
  • Dark background: Add class dk to any .nb-badge or .nb-insured element.
  • Font: Requires DM Sans. Already loaded on all NeoBookworm client sites.
  • Checkatrade stars: Swap 4.9 · 94 reviews for real figures if the client has a Checkatrade profile.
  • Gas Safe reg number: Replace 000000 with the client's actual 6-digit registration number.
